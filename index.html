Our Story

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Results.

The Skypower Group Inc. was founded on a simple principle: security personnel should be as professional, accountable, and dependable as the clients they protect. Over a decade later, that principle guides every contract we sign and every officer we deploy.

We are a full-service private security firm operating across commercial, residential, corporate, and event environments. Our management team brings decades of combined law enforcement, military, and private security experience to every engagement.
